Workspace ONE iOS Excel Add-In (Beta)

Installation

This is the installation documentation page for the Workspace ONE iOS Excel Add-In.


  • Contact Leon Letto for the add-in xml file.

  • Use a sync cable to connect your iPad to your computer. If you're connecting the iPad to your computer for the first time, you'll be prompted with "Trust This Computer". Choose Trust to continue.

  • In iTunes, choose the iPad icon below the menu bar.

  • Under Settings on the left side of iTunes, choose Apps.

  • On the right side of iTunes, scroll down to File Sharing, and then choose Excel in the Add-ins column.

  • At the bottom of the Excel Document column, choose Add File, and then select the manifest.xml file.

  • Open the Excel app on your iPad. If the Excel app is already running, choose the Home button, and then close and restart the app.

  • Open a document.

  • Choose Add-ins on the Insert tab. Your Workspace ONE Add-In is available to insert under the Developer heading in the Add-ins UI.
